Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

seoscore.tools is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: almost none) and how we handle it.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store your website content or scan results on our servers
  • We do not require signup, email, or personal information
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

How Scans Work

When you scan a website, our Cloudflare Worker fetches the page in real-time, runs the analysis, and returns the results directly to your browser. No data is stored on our servers after the scan completes. Your scan history is saved only in your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device.

Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Language preference (your selected language)
  • Scan history (URLs and scores of your past scans)
  • Scan counter (number of scans you've performed)

This data stays on your device and can be cleared at any time by clicking "Clear History" in the footer or clearing your browser data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare Workers — Hosts and serves our application
  • Google Fonts — Provides the Inter and JetBrains Mono typefaces

These services may collect standard web server logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) according to their own privacy policies.

Affiliate Links

Some tool recommendations in scan results may contain affiliate links. These are clearly identified and do not affect your scan results or scores in any way.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at contact@seoscore.tools or visit our About page.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
